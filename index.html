About Shopdi
Shopdi is the first e-commerce platform that uses blockchain technology and NFT to make transaction and product information transparent, putting user experience first, towards building a safe intermediary community for both buyers and sellers to perform transactions and exchange goods.
Ecommerce
Shopdi is an ecommerce platform specializing in high-end products as well as limited edition items, putting user experience first. Shopdi is not only issuing NFTs for limited items but also organizing campaigns to call for pre-orders for trendy products. Besides, through Shopdi's secondary market, users can exchange like-new items.
Blockchain
Shopdi also allows users to buy, sell, pay and transact with a voucher, Shopdi Coin, making cross-border purchases becoming Fast - Convenient - Secure. Moreover, transparency is top respect, especially when applying Blockchain technology to public product details about products as well as charity campaigns.
Shoppertainment
Leading the trend of combining "shopping" and "entertainment", Shopdi focuses on providing enjoyable and funny shopping experiences for buyers by integrating game mechanics into the purchase process on the platform.
